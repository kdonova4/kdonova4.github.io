About Me

I am a graduate of University of North Carolina at Charlotte with a major in computer science. I concentrated in gaming, and artificial intelligence. Several of my courses have directly prepared me for the job of developing a game and collaborating in a group. I worked on projects ranging from game development to software development in a group environment throughout my time at UNCC and that helped me grow my people skills.

While courses I took gave me experience with game design and development, I wasn’t going to let the learning stop there. I have passion for games, and it drives me to create things on my own time. I believe that you can get the best experience when you are working hands on. That’s why I love creating my own little projects in unity or unreal because each project gives me something to learn from and most importantly, experience.
